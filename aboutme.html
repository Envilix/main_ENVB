Nemanja Grebić

High School Student & Aspiring Geneticist

Welcome to my research space. I am a high school student with a deep passion for molecular biology and conservation science. My ultimate goal is to graduate in Genetics and Bioengineering, pursue a PhD, and one day secure a position at Colossal Biosciences to directly contribute to their pioneering work.

I strongly advocate for genetic rescue technologies and closely follow advancements in de-extinction. I believe that modern bioengineering tools are becoming essential resources for restoring degraded ecosystems and reversing anthropogenic ecological damage.

Through this platform, I aim to document my learning journey, share my independent insights, and map out findings regarding growth factors, evolutionary biology, and genetic synthesis.

Furthermore, I intend to use this platform to share my scientific papers, making them highly accessible by describing my findings in a clear, non-scientific style so that anyone can easily understand and connect with the research.
